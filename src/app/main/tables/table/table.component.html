Internal Transfers

These are transfers from other CoinPes Users. Every time they send to your wallet, your account will be credited with a free internal transfer. Since these are off-chain transaction, the transfer is available immediately

Crypto Withdrawals

A crypto withdrawal is a transaction that allows you to move your cryptocurrency balance off the Coinpes platform to an external crypto wallet. This is an 'on-chain' transaction, meaning that your crypto is transferred via the blockchain
