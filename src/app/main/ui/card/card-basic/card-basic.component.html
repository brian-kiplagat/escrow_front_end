Why do i need to confirm the address when sending?

Due to the irreversible nature of cryptocurrency protocols, transactions can't be reversed once initiated. While Coinpes Wallet gives you complete custodian control over your digital assets, this also means that we're not able to recover any funds sent to an incorrect address. We strongly suggest that you double check addresses before sending
